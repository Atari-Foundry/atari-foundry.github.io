How to File a Ticket

  1. Open the issue form via the button above.
  2. Pick the template (bug, feature, discussion) that matches your need.
  3. Fill in emulator/hardware details and attach screenshots or ATRs.
  4. Submit and share the link in the BBS/Discord for faster triage.

  • Use the download vault for the latest demo disks and verification tools.
